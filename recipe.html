RECIPE OF MY FAVOURITE MEAL : BEANS

Origin of Beans

The common bean (Phaseolus vulgaris) originated as a wild vine in Central and South America. Thanks to generations of Indigenous food growers cultivating beans over many millennia, there are thousands of varieties within the P. vulgaris species grown around the world today

S/N Ingredients Quantity
1 Beans 1 tin
2 salt a pinch
3 Water little
4 Onion As prefered

Preparation

  • Pick the beans to remove dirt and uhealthy ones
  • Rinse thoroughly
  • Transfer into boiling water
  • Add little salt
  • Allow to cook till it becomes soft
